Managing Games:
  • You must be logged in to play, view, or manage games.
  • Create a new game from your home base page. Set a password if you want to limit who can play. You can delete this game at any time from your home page. You can have at most one game to your name at a time.
  • Users can join your game by creating a faction on the game view page. You can play in a game you create, but it is not required.
  • When you have at least one faction in your game and you are ready to start, you can have your game begin by pressing "Start Game" from your home base page. This will begin turn 1 of your game and the factions playing it can then access the game play page to play the game.
  • Factions begin each turn set to "In Progress", and when the faction is done giving orders they can set "Turn Complete" from their game play page or from the game view page. Factions that are set to "Turn Complete" can change back to "In Progress" as long as another faction is still "In Progress".
  • When all factions have set "Turn Complete" at the same time, the turn is complete, all orders are executed, and the next turn begin. The owner of the game can also force all factions to be set to "Turn Complete" from their home base page.

Using the play game webpage:
  • Right-click anywhere on the map to recenter it to where ever you click. Click on planets to see information about them. Areas selected in this way will appear bright yellow.
  • Left-click on a planet to set it as "focus", which will stay until you left-click another planet or shift-left-click on empty space. By focusing a planet you can easily order ship launches between planets. Areas focused this way will appear a darker yellow.
  • Spinning the mousewheel up zooms in, and spinning it down zooms out. Holding shift while spinning the mousewheel will zoom faster. There are also buttons to control the zoom if you don't have a mousewheel.
  • Planets you control that have ship parts can launch ships at other planets. If you have such a planet selected or focused information about the ships currently under construction on that planet will appear beside the map, and buttons to order further construction if parts are available.
  • Ships under construction reduce the available parts for further ships, but construction can be cancelled at any time before the ship is completed to reclaim those parts as available. Construction orders appear as blue lines between planets on the map. Ship construction is completed at the end of every turn.
  • If you have two different planets as your focus and selection, if either of those planets can launch ships you will have an option to order a launch at the other planet from the information window beside the map.
